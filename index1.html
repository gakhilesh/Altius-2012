About


ALTIUS 2012 is a vision; a vision envisaged to illuminate the future and enliven the past. It is an effort to search new talents, new geniuses, new intellect, and new brains. With 50+ events ranging from technical to managerial and entertainment to be held in four days, the festival guarantees to be path breaking in its own capacity. The fiesta is organized by the five technical societies of the college representing disciplines TECHNOBYTE, MECHSOC, ELECTRORECK, MICROBUS,MEXPERTS and INFRASTRUCTURE.

Competitions are the mainstay at Altius attracting students from colleges all over India. At a time when engineering talent is hard to come by, Altius offers the sponsors unique oppurtunities for publicity and branding in a vibrant and intellectually stimulating atmosphere.If anything defines the face of Altius, then The spirit of Engineering is the endeavour that showcases it.

With the humble building blocks of innovation, creativity, determination and technique,we intend to craft entities that are record breaking in size and awe inspiring in grandeur. Whether it is a computer geek with a head full of algorithms or a novice looking for intellectual stimulation,Altius has something for everyone.
